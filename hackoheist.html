ABOUT IEEE ADGITM

IEEE (Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers) is the world's largest technical professional organization dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE ADGITM is a student branch under IEEE Region 10 (Asia-Pacific) in the IEEE Delhi section. We are a community of technically active engineering students who aim to learn, share, and create. Our vision is "To make our college campus a learning and growth hub for all its students." We want to inspire students to take up the technical pathway, explore their interests, and create a community of learners and learners. We contribute to this by hosting talks, workshops, camps, and so many other events to foster the learning environment.
