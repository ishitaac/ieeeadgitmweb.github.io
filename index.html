About Us

15+ years of Excellence

IEEE ADGITM is a student branch under IEEE Region 10 (Asia-Pacific) in IEEE Delhi Section. We are a community of technically active engineering students who aim to learn, share and create. Our vision is “ To make our college campus a learn and grow hub for all its students”. We want to inspire and innovate for a technologically advanced future.
